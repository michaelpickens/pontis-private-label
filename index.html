The Opportunity

Gear Is Not An Expense.
It's A Revenue Stream.

Most academy owners treat gear as a cost. The ones building real businesses treat it as a program. Your students need kimonos on day one. Your competition team needs them for every tournament. Your members are walking billboards every time they step on the mat. You're either capturing it or leaving it on the table. We build the program that captures it.

What Separates Us From The Rest.

Factory-Direct. No Middlemen.

After 20 years on the mat and every kimono brand in between, we found the best factory in jiu jitsu. Most technical. Most detailed. Highest quality. We work with them directly so your margins are real and your product shows it.

A Partner, Not A Vendor.

The Pontis Guarantee — any defective item gets replaced. No questions asked. When something goes wrong, we handle it. You don't chase factories. We do. That's the difference between a partner and a supplier.

We Train Too.

We're practitioners and competitors, not consultants. We know how gear needs to fit, move, and hold up over hard trains. That experience is built into every product we touch.

Built To Sell, Not Just Wear.

A great product sitting in a box doesn't make your academy money. We help you presell it, launch it, and sell through it. Inventory management, marketing, email, website. We stay behind the scenes so you can focus on your mats.
